Focused Questioning Reference Template

&nbsp; 🎯 Question 1/5

What is your recruitment target for this year?

&nbsp; Mainly looking to understand:
&nbsp; - How many new HC are planned? (e.g., 200 people, 500 people)
&nbsp; - What types of positions are key? (R&D/Product/Sales/Operations/Functions)
&nbsp; - Is it expansion-oriented hiring or mostly replacement hiring?

&nbsp; This information determines the urgency and resource allocation strategy for company-wide recruitment.
