## 2. Stakeholder Assessment Matrix

### 2.1 Impact vs Influence Analysis

**Impact Level Definitions:**
- **High**: Significant change to work, role, or organization
- **Medium**: Moderate change to processes or tools
- **Low**: Minimal direct impact

**Influence Level Definitions:**
- **High**: Can significantly accelerate or block the change
- **Medium**: Can affect change within their sphere
- **Low**: Limited ability to affect change outcomes

## Appendix: Stakeholder Interview Template

### Interview Questions

**Opening:**
1. What have you heard about [the change] so far?
2. What are your initial thoughts or reactions?

**Awareness:**
3. Why do you think this change is happening?
4. What do you see as the business drivers?

**Desire:**
5. How do you feel about this change personally?
6. What excites you about it? What concerns you?
7. What would make you more enthusiastic?

**Knowledge:**
8. What do you know about how this will affect you/your team?
9. What information do you still need?

**Ability:**
10. What skills or resources would you need to be successful?
11. What barriers do you anticipate?

**Reinforcement:**
12. What would help you sustain the change over time?
13. How should success be recognized?

**Closing:**
14. Is there anything else you'd like to share?
15. Can I follow up with you as we progress?
