**Learning Principles:**
- **Just-in-Time**: Training close to when skills will be applied
- **Blended Approach**: Multiple modalities for different learning styles
- **Practice-Oriented**: Hands-on practice, not just theory
- **Role-Based**: Tailored content by role and needs

## 10. Continuous Improvement

### 10.1 Feedback Collection

**Post-Training Survey Template:**

**Section 1: Reaction**
1. Overall, how satisfied were you with this training? (1-5)
2. How relevant was the content to your role? (1-5)
3. How would you rate the instructor? (1-5)
4. What did you find most valuable?
5. What could be improved?

**Section 2: Learning**
6. I understand what I need to do differently as a result of this training. (1-5)
7. I feel confident in my ability to apply what I learned. (1-5)
8. What questions do you still have?

**Section 3: Suggestions**
9. What additional training or support do you need?
10. Any other feedback?
