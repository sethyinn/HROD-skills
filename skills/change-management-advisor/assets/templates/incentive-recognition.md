## 1. Strategy Overview

### 1.1 Purpose and Objectives

**Purpose:**
Create a comprehensive incentive and recognition system that motivates and sustains change adoption by reinforcing desired behaviors and outcomes.

**Objectives:**
- **Reinforce** the ADKAR model, particularly Desire and Reinforcement dimensions
- **Accelerate** adoption of new behaviors and processes
- **Sustain** change momentum through the transition
- **Celebrate** progress and achievements
- **Align** individual and organizational success

### 1.2 Design Principles

**Key Principles:**
- **Timely**: Recognition soon after the behavior
- **Specific**: Clear about what's being recognized
- **Meaningful**: Relevant to the recipient
- **Fair**: Transparent criteria and equal access
- **Consistent**: Applied reliably across the organization
- **Varied**: Mix of formal and informal, individual and team
