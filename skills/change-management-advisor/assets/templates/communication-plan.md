## 1. Communication Strategy Overview

### 1.1 Communication Objectives
**Primary Objectives:**
- Build **Awareness** of the need for change
- Generate **Desire** to support and participate
- Provide **Knowledge** about what's changing and when
- Enable **Ability** through clear instructions and resources
- **Reinforce** new behaviors and celebrate progress

### 1.2 Key Messages
**Core Message:**
[The single most important message - e.g., "Our new CRM system will help us serve customers better and reduce administrative time."]

**Supporting Messages:**
- [Why the change is happening - the business case]
- [What's changing - scope and timeline]
- [What's in it for me - benefits by audience]
- [How to get help - support resources]

### 1.3 Communication Principles
- **Transparent**: Share both opportunities and challenges
- **Two-way**: Enable dialogue, not just broadcast
- **Timely**: Communicate before changes happen, not after
- **Consistent**: Aligned messages across all channels
- **Segmented**: Tailored to audience needs and concerns

- [Question 1 - e.g., What concerns do you have?]

### 6.3 Feedback Closure Process

1. **Collect**: Gather feedback through various channels
2. **Categorize**: Organize by ADKAR dimension and theme
3. **Prioritize**: High-impact, high-volume items first
4. **Respond**: Address feedback directly when possible
5. **Communicate**: Share what was heard and what's being done
6. **Close**: Confirm resolution and thank contributors
