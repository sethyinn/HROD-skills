\# Feedback Examples



Feedback helps employees improve.



---



\# SBI Feedback Model



Situation

Behavior

Impact



Example:



Situation → yesterday's meeting



Behavior → interrupted customer



Impact → reduced meeting effectiveness



Suggestion → allow client to finish speaking



---



\# Positive Feedback



Situation → customer escalation



Behavior → stayed calm



Impact → customer satisfied



---



\# Difficult Feedback Tips



Managers should:



\* focus on behavior

\* give examples

\* suggest improvement



Avoid:



\* vague criticism

\* emotional reactions
