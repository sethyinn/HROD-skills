\# Hiring Playbook



Hiring the right people is one of the most important responsibilities of a manager.



---



\# Hiring Process



1 Define role

2 Source candidates

3 Structured interviews

4 Evaluate candidates

5 Make hiring decision



---



\# Role Definition



Before recruiting, managers should define:



| Question                          | Purpose            |

| --------------------------------- | ------------------ |

| Why does this role exist          | business need      |

| What problems must be solved      | responsibilities   |

| What results expected in 6 months | success definition |

| What skills required              | hiring criteria    |



---



\# Interview Framework



Use structured interviews.



STAR method:



Situation

Task

Action

Result



Example question:



Tell me about a time you handled a difficult customer.



---



\# Candidate Evaluation



Managers should compare candidates objectively.



| Criteria         | Description        |

| ---------------- | ------------------ |

| Skills           | technical ability  |

| Culture Fit      | team compatibility |

| Learning Ability | growth potential   |

| Experience       | past performance   |



---



\# Hiring Scorecard



| Candidate | Skills | Culture | Growth | Decision |

| --------- | ------ | ------- | ------ | -------- |

| A         | 4      | 5       | 4      | Hire     |

| B         | 5      | 3       | 3      | Discuss  |



---



\# Hiring Checklist



Before making an offer:



\* role clearly defined

\* interview feedback collected

\* candidate references checked

\* compensation aligned with budget



---



\# Common Hiring Mistakes



| Mistake                 | Solution             |

| ----------------------- | -------------------- |

| hiring too quickly      | structured process   |

| focusing only on resume | behavioral interview |

| ignoring culture fit    | team interviews      |
