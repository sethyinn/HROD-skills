# Talent Attraction Playbooks

Attracting strong talent is a critical capability for managers.

Top candidates often choose teams based on

 leadership quality
 growth opportunities
 meaningful work
 team reputation

Managers should actively build their team's attractiveness to potential hires.

---

# Talent Attraction Framework

Effective talent attraction includes four key elements.

1 Team Mission
2 Growth Opportunity
3 Team Culture
4 Manager Reputation

---

# 1 Communicating Team Mission

Strong candidates are attracted to meaningful work.

Managers should clearly communicate

 what the team is building
 why the work matters
 what impact the role will have

Example message

Our team helps customers successfully adopt the product. This role directly impacts customer success and long-term retention.

---

# 2 Highlight Growth Opportunities

Top candidates want to know how they will grow.

Managers should explain

 career development opportunities
 skill development
 potential leadership paths

Example

In this role you will manage customer relationships and gain experience in strategic account management.

---

# 3 Build a Strong Team Culture

Team culture is one of the most important attraction factors.

Managers should promote

 collaboration
 trust
 learning
 recognition

Ways to communicate culture

 share team achievements
 highlight employee growth stories
 demonstrate supportive leadership

---

# 4 Manager Reputation

Candidates evaluate managers as much as companies.

Managers attract talent by

 treating employees fairly
 developing team members
 building strong professional networks
 maintaining a positive reputation

Employees often refer strong candidates to managers they respect.

---

# Talent Attraction Channels

Managers can attract talent through multiple channels.

 Channel                Example                
 ---------------------  ---------------------- 
 Employee referrals     team recommendations   
 Professional networks  LinkedIn               
 Industry events        meetups or conferences 
 Internal mobility      internal candidates    

Employee referrals are often the highest quality source.

---

# Talent Attraction Messaging

Managers should communicate three key messages to candidates.

1 Why this work matters
2 Why this team is great
3 Why this role is a growth opportunity

Example candidate message

This role offers the opportunity to work closely with customers and develop strategic account management skills.

---

# Candidate Experience

Candidate experience strongly influences hiring success.

Managers should ensure

 fast communication
 respectful interviews
 clear expectations
 timely decisions

Poor candidate experience can damage team reputation.

---

# Talent Attraction Checklist

Managers should review these points when attracting candidates.

 role mission clearly defined
 growth opportunities explained
 team culture communicated
 candidate experience positive
 hiring process transparent

---

# Common Talent Attraction Mistakes

 Mistake                       Solution                    
 ----------------------------  --------------------------- 
 focusing only on salary       highlight growth and impact 
 unclear role expectations     define role clearly         
 slow hiring process           communicate timelines       
 poor candidate communication  maintain regular updates    

---

# Talent Attraction KPIs

Managers should monitor talent attraction effectiveness.

 Metric                  Target   
 ----------------------  -------- 
 offer acceptance rate   80%     
 referral hires          30%     
 candidate satisfaction  85%     
 hiring time             45 days 

---

# Example Talent Attraction Message

Example communication to a potential candidate.

Join our team to help customers successfully adopt our platform. You will gain hands-on experience in customer success management and work closely with a supportive and collaborative team.
