\# Onboarding Playbook



The first 90 days determine long-term success.



---



\# 30-60-90 Onboarding Model



30 days → learning

60 days → contribution

90 days → ownership



---



\# First 30 Days



Employee goals:



\* understand company

\* learn team processes

\* meet stakeholders



Manager responsibilities:



\* weekly check-ins

\* onboarding documentation

\* mentor assignment



---



\# 60 Days



Employee should:



\* contribute to projects

\* complete tasks independently



Manager should:



\* review performance

\* provide coaching



---



\# 90 Days



Employee should:



\* work independently

\* integrate with team



Manager should conduct onboarding review.



---



\# Onboarding Checklist



Week 1:



\* team introduction

\* system setup

\* product training



Month 1:



\* first project assignment



Month 3:



\* performance discussion
