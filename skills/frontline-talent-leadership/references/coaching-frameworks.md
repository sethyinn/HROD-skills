\# Coaching Frameworks



Managers should coach employees regularly.



---



\# GROW Model



Goal

Reality

Options

Way Forward



Example:



Goal → improve sales conversion



Reality → conversion rate 15%



Options → better qualification



Way Forward → test new pitch



---



\# One-on-One Meetings



Managers should conduct regular 1-on-1 meetings.



Recommended agenda:



1 progress updates

2 challenges

3 coaching discussion

4 next priorities



Duration:



20–30 minutes



---



\# Coaching Behaviors



Good managers:



\* ask questions

\* listen actively

\* guide thinking



Avoid:



\* micromanagement

\* solving all problems
