# Frontline Talent Leadership

Practical frameworks to help managers manage the full employee lifecycle.

This skill focuses on execution-level management for team leads and middle managers managing teams of 5–30 people.

# When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need help with:

* Hiring team members
* Onboarding new employees
* Setting team or individual goals
* Coaching team members
* Conducting performance reviews
* Delivering performance feedback
* Managing common team issues

---

# Talent Management Lifecycle

Managers should treat people management as a continuous cycle.

Recruit → Attract → Onboard → Goal Setting → Coaching → Evaluation → Feedback → Development

---

# Core Frameworks

### Hiring

Structured hiring process:

1 Define role
2 Source candidates
3 Conduct structured interviews
4 Evaluate candidates
5 Make hiring decision

---

### Onboarding

Use the **30-60-90 onboarding model**

30 days → learning
60 days → contribution
90 days → ownership

---

### Coaching

Use **GROW coaching model**

Goal → Reality → Options → Way forward

---

### Performance Evaluation

Employees should be evaluated based on:

* results
* behaviors
* skills
* growth

---

### Feedback

Use **SBI model**

Situation → Behavior → Impact
